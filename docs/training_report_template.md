## 1. Abstract
- **What to write:** A 200-word summary of the project. Mention the 1.12B architecture, the 300B token pretraining mix (OpenWebMath, FineWeb-Edu), the MaxText TPU setup, and the subsequent SFT/DPO pipeline. Highlight the final performance on GSM8K and MATH.
- **Figures:** None.

## 2. Introduction & Motivation
- **What to write:** Explain *why* you built this. Discuss the value of training from scratch vs fine-tuning (deep understanding of the full stack). Discuss why mathematical reasoning was chosen (easy to evaluate logically, emerging capability). Set the learning objectives.
- **Figures:** A high-level pipeline diagram (Data -> Pretrain -> SFT -> DPO -> Eval).

## 3. Related Work
- **What to write:** Briefly summarize existing small models (TinyLlama, SmolLM2) and math-specific models (DeepSeek-Math, Qwen2.5-Math). Explain how your project synthesizes their approaches on a smaller budget.

## 4. Architecture Design
- **What to write:** Justify the hyperparameter choices (GQA 4:1 for memory bandwidth, 32k vocab for efficiency, bfloat16).

## 5. Data Curation

- **Figures:** A pie chart showing the data mix.

## 6. Tokenizer

- **Tables:** Fertility comparison table (Custom vs Llama 3 vs Qwen).

## 7. Pretraining

## 8. Post-Training: Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT)

## 9. Post-Training: DPO / GRPO

## 10. Evaluation Results

## 11. Ablation Studies
